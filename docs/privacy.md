# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 12, 2026**

## Overview

Orthodox Calendar ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

## Data Collection

**The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically:

- **No account registration** is required
- **No personal information** is collected (name, email, location, etc.)
- **No analytics or tracking** tools are used
- **No data is sent** to any server or third party
- **No cookies** are used
- **No advertising** is displayed

## Local Data Only

All app data is stored locally on your device:

- **Calendar data**: Pre-bundled in the app, not downloaded from the internet
- **Language preference**: Stored locally in iOS UserDefaults
- **Theme preference**: Stored locally in iOS UserDefaults

## Calendar Access

The App requests permission to access your iOS Calendar **only** when you choose to add a feast day reminder. This permission is:

- **Optional**: The app works fully without calendar access
- **User-initiated**: Only requested when you tap "Add Reminder"
- **Write-only**: The app creates calendar events but does not read your existing calendar data

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
